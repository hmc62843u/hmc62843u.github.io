## Point Of View

- what the company believes on this topic:
  - commercialization starts earlier than monetization
  - patents are useful when they make commercial options more legible and credible
  - a patent asset without a clear buyer, partner, or use-case story often underperforms
- what makes this point of view non-generic:
  - it treats patents as business tools, not only legal documents
  - it bridges commercialization with founder authority and public clarity
  - it gives founders a practical lens rather than abstract IP theory
- what the page should explicitly disagree with or clarify:
  - commercialization is not just “license it later”
  - patents do not create business value automatically
  - diligence and trust still depend on explanation, proof, and positioning
