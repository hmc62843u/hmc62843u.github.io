## Point Of View

- what the company believes on this topic:
  - startup patent strategy should begin with business leverage, not paperwork volume
  - patents are useful when they strengthen defensibility, bargaining position, product clarity, or commercialization options
  - filing activity without market logic often produces weak strategic outcomes
- what makes this point of view non-generic:
  - it connects patents to defensibility, positioning, and commercialization
  - it is founder-facing rather than written for in-house counsel or patent specialists
  - it can bridge into W&Patent’s marketplace and Trust Chain work instead of living as isolated legal commentary
- what the page should explicitly disagree with or clarify:
  - not every startup needs the same filing pattern
  - “more patents” is not the same as stronger strategy
  - patents should not be framed only as legal assets; they can also affect leverage, clarity, and buyer confidence
