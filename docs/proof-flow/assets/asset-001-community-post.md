## Draft

One thing I think founder communities often under-discuss is the difference between patent activity and patent strategy.

I put together a short W&Patent case note around an illustrative early-stage B2B scenario. The visible dashboard was not the main strategic asset. The stronger protection target was the workflow logic that made onboarding faster, harder to copy, and easier to defend in diligence, partnership, and commercialization conversations.

That is the lens I keep coming back to: protect the part of the business that changes leverage, not just the part that is easiest to describe.
