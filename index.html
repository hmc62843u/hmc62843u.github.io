The problem

Founders get patent advice designed for big companies

Most patent advice comes from law firms that bill by the hour and assume unlimited budgets. W&Patent starts from a different question: what creates actual leverage for a founder with limited capital and a narrow window?

Commercialization is treated as an afterthought

Filing without a commercialization strategy leaves founders with paperwork instead of leverage. W&Patent connects filing decisions to the business conversations they should support — fundraising, partnership, licensing, and exit.
