Why W&Patent

Founder judgment, not filing volume.

W&Patent exists because most patent advice is designed for big companies with unlimited budgets. Founders need something different: practical judgment on what actually matters enough to protect, calibrated for limited capital and a narrow window.

Not a generic law firm

Law firms bill by the hour and assume unlimited budgets. W&Patent starts from a different question: what creates leverage for a founder who needs to decide fast and spend wisely.

Not a patent broker

Brokers treat every listing like a commodity. W&Patent frames each decision around commercial fit, filing context, and the business conversation it should support.

Not a listing board

Listing boards surface inventory. W&Patent surfaces judgment — what to protect, when, and why it matters for the next stage of the business.

Founded by a patent agent turned entrepreneur

Andrew Leung is a registered patent agent who became an entrepreneur. He understands both sides — the legal system and the founder's need for speed, capital efficiency, and commercial clarity. Read more about Andrew.

OpenFor member context
