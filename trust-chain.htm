Trust Chain is the method W&Patent uses to connect the founder, the company entity, and the proof behind a public claim so the web presence is easier to verify.

Method

Trust is easier to build when the chain is explicit.

Domain

Stable URLs, canonical pages, machine-readable structure, and explicit company identity.

Author

Visible expert authorship, founder context, and a clear relationship between person and company.

Content

Precise claims, citations, proof blocks, and structured answers that are easy to quote and verify.

Who This Is For

  • Founder-led B2B firms with low brand recognition and real expertise
  • Small expert-led companies that need more than a brochure site
  • Teams ready to publish public authority assets on a custom domain

Who This Is Not For

  • Teams looking for instant ranking wins from a template alone
  • Companies with no credible expert, method, or proof to show
  • Businesses that want only gated lead magnets and no public trust surface
Proof Layer

  • method or point-of-view section

  • machine-readable trust signals

What it does not promise

  • guaranteed rankings

  • guaranteed citations without real evidence

What is the Trust Chain?

A way to connect domain, author, and content so a company is easier to verify online.

Does this guarantee GEO or AEO results?

No. It improves structural clarity and proof presentation, which supports better discovery and verification.

Why center the founder?

For unknown firms, a visible expert often makes the company easier to trust than a brand with no human anchor.
