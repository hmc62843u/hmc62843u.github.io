FAQ

Questions about filings, listings, and AI discoverability.

Should I file a provisional first?

Often yes when speed matters and you need an early filing date while refining claim strategy.

How does listing work?

In this static demo, owners choose a plan, preview a signup workflow, and then reveal a mocked dashboard showing how a listing could be managed.

What is a PCT application?

A PCT filing creates a coordinated path for later national filings across participating jurisdictions.

How do buyers engage?

Buyers review the opportunity pages, evaluate fit, and contact W&Patent directly through inquiry links.

How do AI agents discover listings?

They read crawlable listing pages, FAQ answers, JSON-LD, and the static `.well-known/ucp.json` manifest.

Need case-specific guidance?

For filing sequence, jurisdiction strategy, or commercialization framing, contact W&Patent directly.
