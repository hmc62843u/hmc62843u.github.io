FAQ

Common patent filing questions.

Should I file a provisional first?

Often yes when speed matters. A provisional can lock in an early date while you refine claim direction before filing a nonprovisional application.

What is a PCT application?

A PCT filing creates a coordinated path to pursue patent protection across participating jurisdictions through one initial international application.

What is a continuation?

A continuation reuses the parent disclosure while pursuing new claim scope, as long as filing and support requirements are met.

What is a divisional?

A divisional is used when a restriction requirement forces claims into separate inventions, allowing pursuit of non-elected inventions.

What is a continuation-in-part?

A CIP adds new disclosure to existing material. New matter usually receives a later effective filing date than inherited material.
